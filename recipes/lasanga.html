Lasanga Recipe.

simple, but delicous lasanga recipe!

This is your quick an easy lasanga recipe, it involves a wide range of different ingredients where youll be surprised by how authetic and exoctic it tastes, it will give you a huge addiction to lasanga

ingredients

  • Lasanga pasta
  • tomato paste
  • fresh tomatos
  • your favourite spices
  • cheese
  • olive oil
  • your preferred meat

Steps to making lasagna
  1. Get a baking pan
  2. boil lasanga pasta noodles
  3. put tomato paste, crushed tomatos and a bit of olive oil and let it simmer
  4. cook your meat and add it to the tomato sauce
  5. after your cheese is grated, add the pasta noodles, followed by tomato sauce, and then cheese then noodles
  6. repeat until the pan is full, cover with sauce and cheese then bake. after it should be ready in an hour.
