Pizza recipe

simple, but delicous pizza recipe!

This is your quick an easy pizza recipe, it involves a wide range of different ingredients where youll be surprised by how authetic and exoctic it tastes, it will give you a huge addiction to pizza

ingredients

  • pizza dough (or whatever you need to make pizza dough) /li>
  • tomato paste
  • fresh tomatos
  • your favourite spices
  • cheese
  • olive oil
  • your preferred meat

Steps to making pizza
  1. Get a baking pan
  2. make or buy pizza dough
  3. put tomato paste, crushed tomatos and a bit of olive oil and let it simmer
  4. cook your meat and add it to the tomato sauce
  5. after your cheese is grated, add the sauce ontop of a flattened pizza dough piece, followed by cheese and meat.
  6. then bake. after it should be ready in an hour.
