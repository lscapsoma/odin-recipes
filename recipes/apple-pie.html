~ Apple pie recipe

simple, but delicous apple pie recipe!

This is your quick an easy apple pie recipe, it involves a wide range of different ingredients where youll be surprised by how authetic and exoctic it tastes, it will give you a huge addiction to apple pie

ingredients

  • dough (or whatever you need to make pizza dough) /li>
  • apple pie filling
  • cinnamom
  • sugar
  • butter
  • eggs

Steps to making apple pie
  1. Get a baking pan
  2. make or buy dough
  3. put apple pie filling into a bowl and wisk a bit
  4. add your baking pan, roll out your dough and add your filling into it. add some cinnamom and sugar
  5. close it and sprinkle cinnaomon and sugar onto the crush and brush it with butter/eggwash
  6. then bake. after it should be ready in an hour.
